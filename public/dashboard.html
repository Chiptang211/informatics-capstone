Viral Wastewater Detection

Wastewater detection of viruses involves testing sewage for traces of viruses shed by people. It's important because it provides an early indication of viral outbreaks in communities, and can inform you when to be extra careful.

Other Wastewater Contaminants

Other wastewater contaminants may include fungi, bacteria, and hazardous substances. Illicit substance detection in wastewater can inform public health officials about substance abuse trends and facilitate targeted interventions to address these issues effectively.
