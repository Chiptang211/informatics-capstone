About Us

We are a group of Upperclassman Informatics Students studying at the University of Washington. We are completing this project to fulfill our Capstone requirement necessary for graduation. The purpose of our project is to help health-conscious individuals achieve a higher degree of public health literacy. Furthermore, we want health-conscious individuals to be able to utilize location specific health metrics to prevent the spread of infectious diseases. This appllication contains a fully functional front and back end with API/database integration. Data is available to the public at this link: placeholderlink.com
